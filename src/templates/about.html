About DojoPool

Our Mission

DojoPool is dedicated to revolutionizing the way pool players connect, learn, and compete. We combine cutting-edge AI technology with traditional pool gaming to create an immersive and educational experience for players of all skill levels.

Key Features
  • AI-Powered Training: Get personalized feedback and tips from our advanced AI system
  • Community Engagement: Connect with other players and share experiences
  • Tournaments: Participate in exciting tournaments and climb the leaderboard
  • DojoMap: Find pool venues and events near you
Our Story

Founded by passionate pool players and tech enthusiasts, DojoPool emerged from a simple idea: to make pool training more accessible and engaging. Today, we're proud to offer a platform that combines social networking, AI technology, and traditional pool gaming into one seamless experience.
